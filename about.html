Backgroud

Hello, my name is Clyde Ritchie and welcome to my story. I was born and raised in a small town in the nothern part of Virigina, about an hour and half out side of
Washington DC. I went to school here all the way up until college. As a young kid and into my teens I spent all my free time playing different sports throughout the year.
From basketball all the way to cross crountry, I felt like I was always at a school sporting event in my school years. As I came became older and got to the age of a college student I enrolled for
at the first college I attened which was a small school in southern Virigina called Southern Virigina University. During this time I began my studies of computer science and also
played for the schools basketball team. After one year going here I had to transfer to my local community college right outside my hometown. I attened this school for only two semesters
before I moved down to the state of North Carolina. I lived in NC for a few years as I tried to figure out life. During this time I held many different retail jobs
ranging from working in a deli to a hardware store. After living there for three years I moved to Tacoma Washington, which is where I am currently living and enjoying life.
